Tanchiki is a training project for agentic development harness engineering.
The goal is not primarily to build a commercial game.
The goal is to test safe automation patterns before applying them to Ambar.
Automation must increase evidence, not just speed.
Low-risk autonomy is allowed first.
Trust-boundary work requires paired review.
Reviewer identity separation matters.
Human gates remain for merge authority, safety boundaries, and policy changes.
The long-term transfer target is Ambar.
